Who I Am

Like many others I watched computers become so entrenched into our lives, on a scale we've never seen before. And like many kids, I explored that tech such as through the means of video games and youtube. As I got older the question of what made them work got more and more interesting to me. That is why I'm currently studying Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo. Now, I want to use the knowledge that I gain to build innovative technology to help people in their everyday life. From autonomous vehicles to artificial intelligence to exploring Mars, it's only the beginning for computers, and I want to be a part of that story.
