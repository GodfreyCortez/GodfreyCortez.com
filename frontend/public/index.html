Who I Am

Computers have become rooted into our lives to depths never imaginable. Tech got to me at a very young age (mostly through gaming). As I got older the question of how these black boxes made such bright and colourful images became so interesting to me. That is why I'm currently studying Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo. Today, I want to use the knowledge that I gain to build innovative technology to help people in their everyday life. From autonomous vehicles to artificial intelligence to exploring Mars, it's only the beginning for computers, and I want to be a part of that story.
